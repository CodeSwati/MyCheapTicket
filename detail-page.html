Tour Highlights

Shimla is one of the most beautiful hill stations in India, offering a cool climate, scenic views, and a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Visit famous sites like The Ridge, Mall Road, Jakhoo Temple, and the Shimla Heritage Walk.

Included and Excluded

  • 4 nights’ accommodation in Shimla
  • Breakfast and dinner at the hotel
  • All transfers by private vehicle
  • Sightseeing tour of Shimla, including The Ridge, Jakhoo Temple, and Mall Road
  • Helping all traveler for Money Exchange.
  • Visit to Kufri for adventure activities (snow activities in winter)
  • Services of a professional guide
  • Personal expenses like shopping, tips, and extra meals
  • Any activity not mentioned in the itinerary
  • Entrance fees to monuments
  • Travel Insurance

Tour Plan

Arrival in Shimla
  • Arrival in Shimla and transfer to your hotel
  • Relax and enjoy a leisurely walk around The Ridge
  • Explore Mall Road for shopping
  • Overnight stay in Shimla
Shimla Local Sightseeing
  • Visit Jakhoo Temple for scenic views of Shimla
  • Explore Christ Church and Viceregal Lodge
  • Evening at leisure
  • Overnight stay in Shimla
Kufri and Fagu Excursion
  • Drive to Kufri for adventure activities and sightseeing
  • Visit Fagu, a quiet hill station near Shimla
  • Return to Shimla in the evening
  • Overnight stay in Shimla
Shimla Heritage Walk
  • Enjoy a Shimla Heritage Walk, visiting colonial-era buildings and scenic spots
  • Free time in the evening for shopping or relaxation
  • Overnight stay in Shimla
Departure
  • Check out from your hotel and transfer to your departure point
